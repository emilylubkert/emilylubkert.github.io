About Me

I am a full-time student in MIT xPro's Professional Certificate in Coding, focusing on full-stack web development. I worked in education for over 10 years, as a kindergarten teacher, academic dean/school leader, and student-teacher supervisor. I am now transitioning into the world of web development to combine my interests in educational technology with my prior experiences.

I graduated from Duke University with a BA in Public Policy Studies, and I have a Masters in Education from the Harvard Graduate School of Education.

I live in Colorado with my husband, Chris, and our two kids. I love to read, run, ski, eat, and travel.
