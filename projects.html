PacMan Factory

New PacMan images are dynamically added to the screen. They move according to a unique, random velocity and bounce off the boundaries of the window. The image source changes dynamically to show the PacMan changing direction.

Eyes Exercise

The pupils of 2 eyes move to follow the mouse. Users can click a button to change the image, providing what the eyes "need."

Real-Time Bus Tracker

Real-time transit data from the MBTA is shown on a map of Boston. Users can input a bus route and view the current location of each active bus on that route, updated every 5 seconds.
